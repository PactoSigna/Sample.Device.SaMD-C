# Use Specification: Patient — Wearable ECG User

## User Profile

Adults with suspected or diagnosed cardiac arrhythmias who wear a continuous ECG monitoring device. Age range typically 40-85. Variable technical literacy — from smartphone-proficient to minimal technology experience.

## Training and Experience

- **Training Level**: Novice — no medical training assumed
- **Device Training**: Setup instructions provided with device packaging; optional in-clinic onboarding
- **Prior Experience**: May have used fitness trackers or other wearable health devices

## Use Environment

- **Location**: Home, workplace, daily activities
- **Lighting**: Variable (indoor/outdoor)
- **Noise**: Variable (quiet home to busy environments)
- **Distractions**: Daily life activities concurrent with device use

## Physical and Cognitive Characteristics

- May have reduced manual dexterity (elderly patients)
- Vision may be impaired (require readable font sizes)
- May experience anxiety about cardiac condition
- Cognitive load should be minimal — device operates passively

## Use Frequency

Daily — device worn continuously, app checked 1-2 times per day for status.
