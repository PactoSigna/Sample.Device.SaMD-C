# Use Specification: Clinician — ECG Data Reviewer

## User Profile

Cardiologists and primary care physicians who review patient ECG data and detection results. Experienced medical professionals with high domain expertise but limited time per patient review.

## Training and Experience

- **Training Level**: Expert — medical degree with cardiology specialization or primary care training
- **Device Training**: 30-minute orientation session on the web application interface
- **Prior Experience**: Familiar with ECG interpretation, clinical decision support systems, and electronic health records

## Use Environment

- **Location**: Clinic examination room, hospital office, or remote review station
- **Lighting**: Standard office/clinical lighting
- **Noise**: Moderate (clinical environment)
- **Distractions**: Frequent interruptions (pages, other patients, staff questions)

## Physical and Cognitive Characteristics

- High cognitive capacity but limited attention per task
- Reviews many patient records per session (20-40 per day)
- Decision fatigue increases throughout the day
- May delegate initial screening to clinical staff

## Use Frequency

Daily — reviews patient detection results as part of regular clinical workflow. Average review time per patient: 2-5 minutes.
