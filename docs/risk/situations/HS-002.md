# Hazardous Situation: Clinician Dismisses Valid AFib Detection

## Description

A clinician reviewing patient data misinterprets a low confidence score or unclear display, leading them to dismiss a valid AFib detection as unreliable.

## Probability Assessment

**Probability Level: 2 (Remote)**

Factors:

- Confidence display clarity
- Clinician training and experience
- Workload and time pressure

## Contributing Factors

- Ambiguous confidence visualization
- Lack of training on confidence interpretation
- High caseload reducing review time

## Resulting Harm

**Results in:** [HARM-001](../harms/HARM-001.md) - Stroke from Untreated AFib
