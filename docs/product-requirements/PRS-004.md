# Product Requirement: 21 CFR Part 11 Audit Trail

## Description

The system shall maintain a complete audit trail of all data creation, modification, and access events per 21 CFR Part 11 requirements.

## Rationale

FDA regulations require medical device software to maintain audit trails for electronic records. This is a standalone regulatory requirement not derived from user needs.

## Acceptance Criteria

- All data changes logged with timestamp, user, and action
- Audit records immutable (cannot be deleted or modified)
- Audit log accessible to authorized users
- Records retained per data retention policy
